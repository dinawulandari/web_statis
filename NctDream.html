Nct Dream NCT is an abbreviation for Neo Culture Technology. Their first single "Chewing Gum" was released on August 24 and the unit had their debut stage on M Countdown on August 25. The unit consists of seven members: Mark, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Jaemin, Chenle, and Jisung.
