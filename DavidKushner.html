David Kushner is an American music artist, producer, and songwriter. With a passion for creating music and a flair for collaboration, he has become a household name in the music industry. His work has been featured on national television shows and movies, as well as on multiple radio stations. He has worked with some of the biggest names in the industry, including Grammy-award winning artists. David Kushner has an extensive discography from his work over the years. Some of his most notable releases include “Take You Home” with R Kelly (2003), “Yung Bleu - Investments 6” (2020), and “Rich The Kid - Boss Man” (2020). He has also produced tracks for other major artists such as Tyga's “Taste” (2018) and The Roots' “Break You Off” (2002).
