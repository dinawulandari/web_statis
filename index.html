"Ad meliora" is a Latin phrase that means "toward better things". Ad Meliora is a hybrid music-entertainment company, aiming to bring the world's future talents forward.

Founded in 2019, Ad Meliora has seen an exponential growth as a business by harnessing the two most powerful mediums of our age: technology and storytelling with a fan-focused approach to grow the audience and develop a deep relevance for its artists. Today they represent the world's young talents - from Aespa, Nadin Amizah, David Kushner, Gracie Abrams, Hindia, and NCT Dream.

Vision

Lifelong music-making for everyone.

Mission

To be the best global provider of tools and resources for learning, teaching, creating and making music.
