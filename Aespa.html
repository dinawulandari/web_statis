Aespa is a girl group that consists of four members: Karina, Giselle, Winter and Ningning. They debuted on November 17, 2020, with the single "Black Mamba". The group's name, Aespa, combines the English initials of "avatar" and "experience" (Avatar X Experience) with the English word "aspect", meaning "two sides", to symbolize the idea of "meeting another self and experiencing the new world".
