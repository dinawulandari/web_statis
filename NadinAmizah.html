Nadin Amizah (born in Bandung, May 28, 2000) is an Indonesian singer and songwriter. Before starting her singing career, Nadin debuted on one of private TV station programs "Social Media Sensation" in 2016.

Nadin began to be known to the public when she collaborated with Dipha Barus as session singers and vocalist in the song All Good in 2017.
